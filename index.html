About Me

In 2017 I graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelors of Science degree in Applied Physiology and Kinesiology with a specialty in Exercises Physiology. I am a certified personal trainer by the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM-CPT) and a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist by the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA-CSCS). Currently, I am pursuing a Masters Degree in Dietetics and Nutrition at Florida International University to become a Licensed and Registered Dietitian (LDN/RDN).

My interest in training and nutrition began at a very young age in middle school. I didn’t grow up in the best household environment and training/healthy eating really helped me through all the tough times. I realized how much of a positive impact this had on me and since then I wanted to help everyone around me improve their lives through fitness and nutrition. I want everyone to be able to feel what it’s like to be the best version of themselves. I want to show people that the hard work they put into training and eating well will also translate positively to everything they do on a day to day basis. Lastly, I want to give the confidence and power for people to overcome any obstacle they may face in life. I am a firm believer and advocate for preventive medicine. Several health issues in this world can be prevented or cured with proper care of oneself. I am very passionate about what I do and would love the opportunity to help you reach your goals in life.

Aside from my educational and professional achievements, I have competed in a few spartan races, played varsity tennis and basketball for 4 years. I have also competed in the crossfit open for 4 years which led me to transition to Olympic weightlifting. In my junior year of college, I successfully qualified for the university nationals in Olympic lifting. Eventually, I took a break from lifting to change up my routine and got into swimming and hit a personal goal of swimming a mile. Today, I like to crosstrain and incorporate a large variety of resistance and endurance training and make sure I am always having fun. During my downtime, I love spending time outdoors with my dog, Henry, or at the beach with family.
